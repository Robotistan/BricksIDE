Magic Lamb

In this project, we will turn a lamp we use at home on and off with sound. In our project, which we will build using the Picobricks sound level sensor, we will perform the on-off operations by making a clap sound.

Most of us have seen lamps flashing magically or doors opening and closing with the sound of clapping in movies. There are set assistants who close these doors and turn off the lamps in the shootings.
